Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 1 hr 30 min

  • Preparation: 35 min

  • Cooking: 55 min

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Yogur
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Herbs
  • Green Chilies
  • Oil and Ghee
  • Lemon juice
  • Saffron

Instructions

  1. Marinate the meat with yogurt and spices.
  2. Fry the onions until golden brown and set aside.
  3. Cook the meat with tomatoes and spices until tender.
  4. Garnish with fried onions, herbs, and saffron (if using).
  5. Dum cook (slow cook) on low heat until fully cooked.
